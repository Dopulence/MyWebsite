The History
of D'OPULENCE

Founded in 2017 in Patna, Bihar by 3 musketeers, D'OPULENCE started its journey with a single outlet to serve the trend lovers for all their hair and skin needs. Following the spectacular response of the visiting customers further gave us inspiration to extend our boundary and start multiplying.

Today D'OPULENCE has become the single haircare and fashion destination serving 3 branches in Patna and on the verge of adding more in Patna and expanding further destination across India.
