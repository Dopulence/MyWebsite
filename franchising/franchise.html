Franchise Opportunities

Whether you are looking to start with a single-unit operation, or desire a multi-unit operation, we have the franchise opportunity that is right for you in markets across the United States. We help you with site selection, offer custom design, initial and ongoing training for your staff, and provide operational guidance as you start your D'OPULENCE Hairdressing Salon.

Salon Layout

The D'OPULENCE strategy is based on simplicity: an easy-to-follow business model with a strong operating system and world-class educational system.

Our goal is simple

We provide each client with a highly educated hair stylist who can make them look good and feel good. We establish relationships with satisfied clients who return often and recommend us to friends and family.

Our revenue model is based on providing clients with price points that fit every budget. We want to be a salon for the entire family. We focus on multiple touch points throughout the client visit to enhance their experience, with an emphasis on educating the client on how to maintain or reproduce the look at home, including prescribing tools and products for use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose D'OPULENCE for your salon?

Franchisees choose D'OPULENCE to be a part of an internationally recognized iconic brand that has been developed over the last 7 years and for our reputation for quality education and industry leading hairdressing techniques. Some choose D'OPULENCE because of our simple business system and operational model, which makes owning and running a single salon, or multiple salons, as streamlined as possible.

What qualifications does D'OPULENCE look for in potential franchisees?

We look for like-minded individuals who want to be a part of an iconic hairdressing family brand and help continue our vision of advancing the hairdressing industry, one hairdresser at a time.

  • Do you have what it takes?
  • Are you passionate about the success of your team and your salon?
  • Do you have proven management and leadership skills?
  • Are you looking to adopt a proven operating system completely and consistently?
  • Do you have a minimum net worth in liquid assets?
  • Do you have the ability to finance?
How much money can I make?

D'OPULENCE offers its franchisees strong unit-level economics. Potential franchisees can review the financial information in our Franchise Disclosure Document during their due diligence process. The Franchise Disclosure Document will assist you in developing your own financial projections and business plan. How much you can make depends on many factors, including but not limited to the following:

  • How well you satisfy your customers’ needs by providing excellent customer service
  • How thoroughly, consistently, and faithfully you implement all of D'OPULENCE’s operating principles
  • How well you recruit and develop an education-focused staff
  • How effectively you monitor your sales trends while managing your expenses
  • How you address competition in your area
  • Regulations and laws specific to the industry and to your particular location
  • Economic factors which may not be within your control
If I live outside Bihar, can I franchise with D'OPULENCE?

D'OPULENCE is open to franchising in East, West, North, South and Central India. If you are interested in exploring currently available options, please fill out our franchise inquiry form to speak with a franchise development specialist.

What types of ongoing training & support are there?

D'OPULENCE offers corporate marketing and business operational support from the moment your salon doors open. We assist by providing the following:

  • Marketing collateral
  • Marketing & PR concepts
  • Internal systems support
  • Internal communications support
  • A designated or approved POS system provider
  • Branding
  • Online manual access
  • Intranet systems
  • Access to the D'OPULENCE App
  • Initial and ongoing training programs
  • National Collection Release, Salon Summit & Photographic Awards
  • And more…

*All details will be mentined in the Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)

If I already own my own salon, can I convert it to a D'OPULENCE Salon?

Absolutely. If you have an existing salon that you would like to convert to a D'OPULENCE salon and capitalize on the model and systems we have developed, fill out our franchise inquiry form and our franchise development specialist will contact you.
